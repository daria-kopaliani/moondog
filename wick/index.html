Wick: Candle Maker's Calculator

Every number you need to pour a candle — in one place, instead of a stack of web pages.

Guides

  • How much wax and fragrance for a candle?

    Size the wax to the vessel, the fragrance to the wax — and the two limits (wax max and IFRA) that keep it safe.

  • How to choose a candle wick size

    Pick a wick series for your wax, size it to the container diameter, adjust for fragrance and dye, then test-burn.

What Wick calculates

Enter your vessel and wax, and Wick gives you the wax needed by vessel volume and container count, the fragrance load for your target percentage with a guardrail that warns when you exceed the wax's max or the fragrance's IFRA limit, a starting-point wick size by container diameter, and the per-candle cost of your batch. No subscription, no ads, works offline.
