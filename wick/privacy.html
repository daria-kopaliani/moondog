Wick: Candle Maker's Calc — Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-06-12

The short version

Wick does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your recipes and preferences stay on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no third-party trackers. We do not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, or usage data.

What we store on your device

The recipes you save and your preferences are stored locally using Apple's on-device storage. This data stays on your iPhone or iPad, is never transmitted off your device, is included in your iCloud device backup if you have that enabled, and is deleted when you delete the app.

In-app purchases

Wick is free with a 48-hour trial. A single one-time, non-consumable in-app purchase ($6.99) unlocks the app permanently — no subscriptions, no recurring charges. The purchase is handled entirely by Apple; we never see your payment details.

Network & health data

The app makes no network calls and does not integrate with HealthKit. There are no servers we operate.

Safety

Wick is an educational craft calculator. Its numbers (wax weight, fragrance load, wick size) are cited starting points from CandleScience, The Flaming Candle, IFRA Category 12 limits, and wax-manufacturer specs — not guarantees. Candle making involves open flame and hot wax; always burn-test your own candles.
