Reef: Aquarium Chemistry — Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-06-04

The short version

Reef: Aquarium Chemistry does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything you enter (tank volume, test readings, dose logs, preferred products) stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers. We do not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, or usage data.

What we store on your device

The app saves your tank setup (volume, salinity target, preferred products, unit preferences), your test logs (calcium, alkalinity, magnesium, nitrate, phosphate, salinity, temperature readings), and your dose logs locally on your device using SwiftData. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is not transmitted anywhere
  • Is removed if you delete the app

What we share

Nothing. We have nothing to share because we collect nothing.

Third-party services

None. Reef: Aquarium Chemistry does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, including analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or AI providers.

Network access

The app does not require an internet connection to function. It does not make network requests during normal use.

Children's privacy

Reef: Aquarium Chemistry is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored off your device, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. To remove all locally stored app data, delete the app from your device.

Changes to this policy

If the app changes in a way that affects this policy, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
