Garage: Car Care & Service Log

Everything a car owner has to remember — offline, and yours forever.

What it does

Garage keeps a complete, private log for every vehicle you own: fuel fill-ups with real full-tank fuel-economy trends, your whole service/repair/mod history, and predictive maintenance reminders by mileage or time — oil, tires, brakes, fluids, timing belt — with editable normal and severe intervals. Add a car by VIN (free NHTSA decode) or by hand.

It all stays on your device. When it's time to sell, export a clean PDF or CSV service record that proves how well the car was kept — the history that pays you back at resale. Multiple vehicles, export, and custom reminders unlock with a one-time purchase; one vehicle and core logging are free. No subscription.
