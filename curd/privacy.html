Curd: Cheese Making Calc — Privacy Policy

Effective 2026-06-13

The short version

Curd does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything you enter (saved makes, aging logs, notes, unit preferences) stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers. We do not collect your name, email, location, device identifiers, or usage data.

What we store on your device

The app saves your aging log (each "make": cheese style, milk volume, make date, completed care tasks, reminder preference, and your notes) and your unit preferences locally on your device using SwiftData and the system preferences store. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Is not transmitted anywhere
  • Is removed if you delete the app

Notifications

If you turn on flip/wax reminders for a make, Curd schedules local notifications on your device. These are generated and delivered entirely on-device; nothing is sent to a server, and the app uses no push-notification infrastructure.

In-app purchases

Curd offers a one-time Curd Unlock purchase ($6.99) that adds the aging log and recipe scaling; the six calculators are free. Purchases are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not see your Apple ID, payment method, or billing information — Apple handles all of that under their own privacy policy. The app only learns whether a successful purchase is associated with the active Apple ID on this device.

What we share

Nothing. We have nothing to share because we collect nothing.

Third-party services

None. Curd does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, including analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or AI providers.

Network access

The app does not require an internet connection to function. The only network calls it can make are to Apple's StoreKit servers (to fetch the unlock price or restore a prior purchase) and to outbound links you tap (the cited source pages, which open in Safari). No other network requests are made.

Children's privacy

Curd is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Since no personal data is collected or stored off your device, there is nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. To remove all locally stored app data, delete the app from your device.

Changes to this policy

If the app changes in a way that affects this policy, we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
